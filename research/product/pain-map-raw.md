# PhD Pain Map — Raw Quotes

> 55 real quotes from researchers about their workflow pain points.
> Source: Reddit, HN, Zotero Forums, ResearchGate, Thread Reader.
> Collected: Feb-Mar 2026.

See full content in sprint zero research document.

## Clusters (frequency in sample of 55)

| Cluster | Count | Key signal |
|---------|-------|------------|
| Overload and anxiety | 18/55 | "can't keep up", "drowning", "no catching up" |
| Tool friction and sync | 12/55 | Crashes, lock-in, annotations don't sync |
| Organization and retrieval | 8/55 | "messy library", duplicates, lost highlights |
| Related work and synthesis | 7/55 | Enumeration vs narrative, scope creep, plagiarism anxiety |
| Prioritization and rabbit holes | 6/55 | 50 tabs, citation chasing, no stopping criteria |
| Gaps and originality | 4/55 | Can't judge value, can't formulate "the real gap" |

## Strategic insight

> The pain is not "lack of tools". It's **lack of continuity between discovery → triage → reading → synthesis → writing**, with loss of context and decisions at every handoff.
